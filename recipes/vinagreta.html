Vinagreta recipe

An ideal sauce to acompany your meat dishes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Olive oil (125 ml)
  • 1/2 cup vinegar (125 ml)
  • 1/2 teaspoon grounded black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. In a cover bowl, mix all the ingredients.
  2. Put the bowl in the freezer at least two hour before use
